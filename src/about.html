About Us

History

Our mission is to rent cars for tourists that want to visit Ethiopia. Hayktur first started in 2005 as a tourist guide company.

Haykel, with other family members started to notice the lack of roads in many place in the country. Due to the lack of vehicles, many magnificent places were out of reach for most tourists. After noticing this gap, Hayktur pivoted to renting vehicles to tourists in 2010.
